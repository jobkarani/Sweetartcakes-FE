You can often find Hayley working from her garden studio crafting elaborate sugar flower forms or indulging in her other passion, interior design. When she’s not creating extraordinary masterpieces for weddings, Hayley loves nothing more than spending time with her husband, two children (D’Arcy and Harrison) and Dexter, the beagle.

Before Hayley’s journey to becoming a wedding cake designer, she was a medic in the Army, which immersed her into an exhilarating and endlessly exciting world. Hayley’s determination, resilience and grit have led her to where she is today, establishing herself as a luxury wedding cake designer and becoming distinguished within the industry for her exceptional sugar flower and wafer paper skills.

Hayley’s warm, genuine and personal approach promises impeccable service without compromising on luxury. From the moment you arrive at your cake tasting consultation, you will feel like you have known each other forever. The beginning of an experience that you will never forget.

Hayley’s devotion to exercise began after her car accident, filling her free time with running to fuel a positive mindset. With the New Forest on her doorstep, she is often racking up the miles with her dog, Dexter. With an unrelenting love for coffee and an adoration for all things pink, if you could find Hayley anywhere in the world, it would be soaking up the scenery on a Cornish coastline while sipping a glass of champagne – the ultimate luxury.

Hayley’s refined style is recognised across the wedding industry; it’s understated yet elegant, with intricate artwork and visually stunning details that need to be seen to be believed. The flavours are given just as much consideration as the design, with a selection of pairings that will immerse your palate in an array of aroma and taste.
